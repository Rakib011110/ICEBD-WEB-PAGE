Bangladesh

ICE Bangladesh was established in [Year of Establishment]. It's one of the largest regions outside the UK with more than [Number] members.

The Regional Board, Bangladesh Association (ICE BD), Graduates & Students Division (G&S), and Regional Support Team work together to further the objectives of the Institution and to serve our members' interests in Bangladesh.

We offer membership advice, and help run a huge range of events and training opportunities to support ICE members in the region.

ICE Breaker is the newsletter in electronic format to be distributed to all members in the Bangladesh Region. The current version of ICE Breaker [Current Year] summarises events and activities in ICE BD in the past 12 months.

To find out about events in Bangladesh, please visit our social media channels:

  • ICE Bangladesh Instagram
