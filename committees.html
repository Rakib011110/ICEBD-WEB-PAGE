ICE Bangladesh's committees bring together like-minded people and give members the chance to help influence and shape our work.

As well as influencing our priorities, committees work to create an annual programme of events, ranging from technical lectures to visits to ongoing projects and other sites of interest to civil engineers.

The region is made up of the Bangladesh Executive Board, Bangladesh Regional Committee and the Bangladesh Graduates and Students Committee.

Committee members are volunteers and come from a range of backgrounds within the civil engineering profession.

Regional committee

The Regional Board is charged with furthering the objectives of ICE in Bangladesh. It is responsible for giving guidance and advice to the Bangladesh Association (BDA) and the Regional Support Team, and setting strategy for the RST in supporting our activities.

Regional board members

The Chairman of the Bangladesh Regional Board is the ICE Representative for Bangladesh. Other members of the Board and their specific duties are:

  • Bangladesh Representative on Council - responsible for facilitating communication between ICE Bangladesh with ICE's International Policy Committee and the Council
  • Chairperson of BDA - responsible for leading BDA to promote learned society activities
  • Regional Director for Bangladesh - responsible for leading the RST to promote membership, administer & control high profile projects and, as resources allow, support the activities of BDA.

The Regional Board can also invite eminent ICE members to act as Honorary Advisors.

BDA G&S committee

The Graduates and Students Division (G&S) is a sub-division of the Institution of Civil Engineers Bangladesh Association (BDA). Comprising recent graduates and current students embarking on a civil engineering career in Bangladesh, the G&S emphasises both professional and personal development and connects graduate and student members with the industry.

Graduate & student committee members

BDA G&S prides itself on its variety of opportunities, spanning site visits, hands-on workshops, enrichment seminars, networking opportunities, overseas delegations and much more. We spare no effort in promoting civil engineering among the next generation, with initiatives covering industry visits, experiential learning, idea pitching and career talks. G&S also maintains strong ties with young committees of local professional institutions and local branches of reputable institutions.

G&S delivers professional development and networking opportunities through its various event teams.

Delegation team

  • Overseas delegations for members to appreciate engineering best practices overseas and promote ICE to overseas professionals
  • Sharing sessions where delegates share and consolidate their takeaways from delegations

External engagement team

  • Joint events with other professional bodies, covering both professional development and networking
  • The flagship Shaping Our Future City campaign that aims to promote civil engineering profession to high secondary school students

Personal development team

  • Various workshops on personal wellbeing, professional ethics and other soft skills
