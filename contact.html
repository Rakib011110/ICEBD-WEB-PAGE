ICE Bangladesh works to support our members in the region and to raise the profile of civil engineering.

The Institution of Civil Engineers (ICE) Bangladesh Chapter is a professional organization dedicated to supporting civil engineers and infrastructure development in Bangladesh. .

It is part of the global ICE network, which unites civil engineers worldwide to promote professional excellence, knowledge sharing, and the advancement of civil engineering practices.
