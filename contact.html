Contact

ICE Bangladesh works to support our members in the region and to raise the profile of civil engineering.

To get involved with awards, or for support on fellowship, AMICE, events and wider stakeholder working, the Regional Support Team is here to help.

We'll always respond as quickly as possible, although some staff work part-time.

The Membership Recruitment Team will guide and advise you on any membership related queries.

Follow us on social media and keep up to date with all the latest from your region.
